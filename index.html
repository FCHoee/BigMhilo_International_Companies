History and Milestones

  • 2005: Founding - BigMhilo international company was founded with a vision to revolutionize the freelance industry by providing a platform that connects talented freelancers with businesses in need of their expertise. The journey began with a small team dedicated to creating opportunities for freelancers to thrive and businesses to succeed.
  • 2008: Platform Launch - In 2008, BigMhilo International officially launched its user-friendly platform, offering a seamless way for freelancers and businesses to connect. The platform's intuitive design and robust features quickly gained traction, attracting both freelancers and businesses from various industries.
  • 2012: Expansion and Growth - As the demand for freelance talent grew, so did BigMhilo International companies. In 2012, we expanded our reach to international markets, establishing partnerships with freelancers and businesses worldwide. This expansion marked a significant milestone in our journey towards becoming a global leader in freelance recruitment.
  • 2016: Industry Recognition - In 2016, BigMhilo International companies received industry recognition for its innovative approach to freelance recruitment. We were honored with prestigious awards for our commitment to excellence, customer satisfaction, and contribution to the freelance community. These accolades reaffirmed our position as a trusted partner for freelancers and businesses alike.
  • 2020: Milestone Achievements - In 2020, BigMhilo International companies reached a major milestone by successfully matching over 10,000 freelancers with businesses across diverse sectors. This achievement showcased our dedication to facilitating mutually beneficial collaborations and driving success for our users.
  • Present Day: Continued Success - Today, BigMhilo International Company continues to evolve and adapt to the ever-changing landscape of the freelance industry. With a growing community of freelancers and businesses, we remain committed to connecting talent with opportunity, fostering innovation, and driving growth for all our users.
